What is APMI?

APMI, which stands for "Animals Playing Musical Instruments," is a brand created for the person who just so happens to treasure the awe-inspring idea of animals having the intellectual and emotional capability to play musical instruments (sounds awesome, right?). Our apparel is casual, yet stylish, and so very comfortable. With all designs custom printed onto Comfort Colors blanks by each member of the APMI team, you are guarenteed to love the feel and design of our shirts.

Who Are We?

APMI is a family owned and family run apparel business, and the founding members are Parker Guest, Ben Guest, Charlie Guest, Luke Guest, and Noah Slack. Follow us on Instagram @apmibrand!
